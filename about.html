ABOUT US

GAMESCOM IS A PLATFORM FOR AMERICAN DIGITAL CREATIVES , CELEBRATING THEIR WORKS AND CONNECTING THEM TO OPPORTUNITIES FOR LEARNING, . COLLABORATION AND FUNDING..

GAMESCOM is a platform that brings together enthusiasts and digital creatives across the games, animation, comics, and movie industry in America via insightful events such as conventions, game jams and so much more. Our aim is to invite investments into the space and foster collaboration among digital creatives across America as well as celebrating the rich and amazing level of creativity possessed by Americans.
